Learn more about the issue

Access to safe and affordable drinking water is enshrined in California's Human Right to Water Legislation. However, as of 2018 an estimated 1 million residents in California do not have access to safe drinking water, including a number within the Sacramento-San Joaquin Delta (Delta). Safe drinking water issues in the Delta include limited funding for local water systems, arsenic contamination, and other localized contamination issues.
